Geometry-Dash energy, terminal palette. The cube auto-runs - you just jump (and
double-jump). Dodge spike triangles, don't face-plant into block sides, and hit
amber pads for a big launch. Speed ramps the further you get.

One HTML file, one canvas, no build step. Collect `$` tokens if you're feeling greedy.
